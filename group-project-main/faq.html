Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I volunteer for CCC?

You can volunteer by joining! Each time a show is about to happen, the club will send out a volunteer form to all members! To join, come to McGraw Hall room 165 on any Thursday at 5:00pm for a G-Body meeting!

  • How can I submit an artist proposal to CCC?

If you want to suggest an artist, fill out the artist suggestion form! It can be found here!

  • How can I stay up-to-date on CCC events?

Following our instagram is the best way to stay up-to-date on large-scale club events, but for smaller things such as community events, attending weekly club meetings is the best way to stay involved.

  • How can I give feedback to CCC?

If you have accessibility-related feedback, you can fill out the accessibility form in our linktree. If you have other feedback, you can DM us on instagram or email the relevant eboard member.

Concert Rules and Safety

Cornell Concert Commission primarily hosts concerts in two different venues:

Barton Hall and Bailey Hall.

Barton Hall

Entrance at the front doors ONLY.

Only small bags are allowed, and they will be searched at the door.

Food and Drinks are not permitted in Barton Hall.

Bailey Hall

Entrance at the front doors ONLY.

Bags are not permitted and will be subject to search if they are brought.

Food and Drinks are not permitted in Bailey Hall.
