Join us for a Harvest Extravaganza!

Discover the essence of fall at the Apple Harvest Festival—an immersive celebration of all things apple and autumn. From September 30 to October 2 (Friday, Saturday, and Sunday), the vibrant Ithaca Commons transforms into a haven for apple enthusiasts and festival-goers alike. Indulge in farm-fresh apples and produce, savor delectable apple-infused baked goods, and explore a variety of food trucks offering mouthwatering bites. The craft fair showcases artisans from across the region, displaying unique creations that capture the spirit of fall.

A Symphony of Flavors and Entertainment

Experience the festival's lively atmosphere with live music and entertainment throughout the weekend. Follow the cider trail to discover refreshing cider options, adding a spirited twist to your apple-filled adventure. Inside shops in and around Downtown, find a treasure trove of apple-inspired products for sale, ensuring you take a piece of the festival's charm home with you.

Save the Dates: September 30 to October 2

Mark your calendars for this harvest extravaganza! Whether you're a local or a visitor, join us on the iconic Ithaca Commons for a weekend filled with the crisp delights of fall, live entertainment, and a community celebration like no other. The Apple Harvest Festival awaits—where the spirit of autumn comes alive!
