Music

I kicked off my piano journey at the tender age of 4, and what a whirlwind it's been! Music turned out to be my jam, big time! I've got this nifty thing called "absolute pitch" that lets me ID any note, and my radar for pitch, notes, and music vibes is just uncanny.

Back in primary school, I jumped into proper singing training and boy, did I snag a bunch of wins in competitions. Middle school came around, and I hopped onto the Bel Canto train as a Soprano. Check out my piano performance on my YouTube Channel at Xiaokun Du -Youtube.

Guitar came into the picture after middle school, and guess what? I brought my fave guitar all the way from London to Ithaca this fall. It's been a groovy ride, and I'm just getting started!

Mancity Fan

For the past four years, I've proudly supported the Manchester City football team. Since beginning my journey at a UK university, I've had the incredible opportunity to witness their matches live on numerous occasions. Allow me to share some of these unforgettable experiences with you!

Manchester City vs PSG

During the group stage of the UCL on November 24, 2021, I had the thrilling chance to attend the game at the Etihad Stadium, Manchester City's home. The excitement was palpable as I watched my beloved team triumph over PSG. The atmosphere was electric, with jubilation all around. I even had the surreal pleasure of meeting some of my favorite players in person!

Manchester City vs Inter Milan

On June 10, 2023, the stage was set for one of the most riveting and nerve-wracking matches‚ÄîManchester City against Inter Milan. Though I couldn't be there in person, I watched the game at a London pub, wearing my Mancity shirt. When we emerged victorious in the UCL final, tears of joy streamed down my face. This was a dream I had cherished for so long, and it finally came true.
